About SmileyPDF

Welcome to SmileyPDF! Our mission is simple: to provide easy-to-use, free, and secure online tools for all your basic PDF needs. We believe that managing PDF documents shouldn't require expensive software or complicated installations.

Our Focus: Security & Simplicity

In today's digital world, privacy is paramount. That's why SmileyPDF is built with a client-side processing approach. This means that when you use our tools (like the editor, merger, or converter), your files are processed directly within your web browser on your own computer.

Your files are never uploaded to our servers. This significantly enhances security and privacy, as your sensitive documents never leave your control. Use our tools with peace of mind.

We strive to make our tools intuitive and straightforward. Whether you need to quickly add a signature, merge multiple reports, convert an image, or extract text, our goal is to make the process hassle-free.

Our Tools

We offer a growing suite of free online tools designed for everyday PDF tasks:

  • PDF Editor: Add text, images, signatures, and highlights.
  • Merge PDF: Combine multiple PDF files into one document.
  • Image to PDF: Convert JPG, PNG, WEBP images to PDF format.
  • PDF Rotator: Rotate individual or all pages within a PDF.
  • Extract PDF Text: Pull text from PDFs, with OCR for scanned images.
  • PDF to JPG: Convert each PDF page into a JPG image.
  • PDF to Word: Get editable text from PDFs into a .docx file (basic formatting).

Why Free?

We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone. SmileyPDF is supported by unobtrusive advertising, which allows us to keep the tools completely free without requiring user sign-ups, subscriptions, or payments for basic features.

Thank you for choosing SmileyPDF! We hope our tools make working with documents easier and more secure.
