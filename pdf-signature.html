How to Add Digital Signatures to PDFs

  1. Upload your PDF document using the drag & drop area or the "Select PDF File" button.
  2. Create your signature using one of three methods:
    • Draw: Use your mouse or touchscreen to draw your signature.
    • Type: Type your name and convert it to a signature.
    • Upload: Upload an image of your existing signature.
  3. Customize your signature color if desired.
  4. Click "Add to Document" to place your signature on the PDF.
  5. Position the signature where you want it on the document.
  6. Download your signed PDF document.

About Digital Signatures:

This tool creates visual signatures that look like handwritten signatures. For legally binding electronic signatures with cryptographic verification, please consult a specialized e-signature service.
