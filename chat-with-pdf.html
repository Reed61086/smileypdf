Frequently Asked Questions

How does the PDF Chat feature work?

Our PDF Chat uses advanced AI to analyze your document directly in your browser. It extracts and processes the text, enabling you to ask questions and receive accurate answers based on the content of your PDF.

Is my PDF data secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens directly in your browser using client-side technology. Your PDF never leaves your device or gets uploaded to our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

What types of questions can I ask?

You can ask virtually any question about the content of your PDF. This includes summary questions, specific details, explanations of concepts, relationships between ideas, and more. The AI is designed to understand context and provide relevant answers.

Are there any file size limitations?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the file size limit depends on your device's memory. Most modern devices can easily handle PDFs up to 100MB, but performance may vary for very large files.
