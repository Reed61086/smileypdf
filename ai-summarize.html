Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the AI-generated summaries?

Our AI summarization tool uses advanced natural language processing to create highly accurate summaries. While no automated summary can perfectly capture every nuance, our tool excels at identifying and extracting the most important information from documents.

Is my PDF data secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens directly in your browser using client-side technology. Your PDF never leaves your device or gets uploaded to our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

What types of documents work best with the summarizer?

Our summarizer works well with most text-based PDF documents, including academic papers, business reports, legal documents, articles, and books. Documents with clear structure and primarily textual content yield the best results.

Are there any file size limitations?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the file size limit depends on your device's memory. Most modern devices can easily handle PDFs up to 100MB, but performance may vary for very large files.
