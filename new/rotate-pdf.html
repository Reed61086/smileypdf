How to Rotate PDF Pages Online

  1. Upload PDF: Select the PDF file you need to rotate.
  2. Preview Pages: Thumbnails of your pages will appear.
  3. Select Rotation: Click the rotation buttons (e.g., Rotate Left 90°, Rotate Right 90°, Rotate 180°) for individual pages or use the 'Rotate All' options if available.
  4. Apply Changes: Click the 'Apply Rotation' or 'Save' button.
  5. Download: Get your PDF with the corrected page orientations.

Why Rotate PDF Pages?

  • Fix Scan Orientation: Correct pages that were scanned upside down or sideways.
  • Adjust Landscape/Portrait: Change the orientation of specific pages within a document.
  • Prepare for Printing: Ensure all pages are oriented correctly before printing.
  • Improve Readability: Make documents easier to read on screen.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Rotating PDFs

Can I rotate just one page in a multi-page PDF?
Yes, our tool typically allows you to preview all pages and apply rotation selectively to individual pages or to the entire document at once.
Will rotating affect the quality of my PDF?
No, rotating pages is a lossless operation. The text and image quality of your document will remain unchanged.
