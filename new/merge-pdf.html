How to Merge PDF Files Online

  1. Upload Your PDFs: Click the 'Select PDF Files' button or drag and drop the PDF files you want to combine into the upload area. You can select multiple files at once.
  2. Arrange the Order: Once uploaded, your files will appear in a list. Drag and drop the file previews to arrange them in the exact order you want them to appear in the final merged document.
  3. Start Merging: Click the 'Merge PDFs' button. SmileyPDF will process your files and combine them into one single PDF.
  4. Download Your File: After a few moments, a download link for your newly merged PDF file will appear. Click it to save the document to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Merging PDFs

Is there a limit to the number of files I can merge?
While we aim to be flexible, there might be practical limits based on server capacity or total file size. For typical use, merging a reasonable number of files (e.g., up to 10-20) should work smoothly. If you encounter issues with a large number of files, try merging them in smaller batches.
Can I reorder the files before merging?
Absolutely! After uploading, simply drag and drop the file previews into your preferred sequence before clicking the 'Merge PDFs' button.
Will the quality of my PDFs be affected?
Our merging process is designed to preserve the original quality and layout of your documents as much as possible. The merged PDF should look just like the individual files combined.
Are my uploaded files safe?
Yes. We prioritize your security and privacy. Files are transferred over secure HTTPS connections and are automatically deleted from our servers within an hour after processing. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.
