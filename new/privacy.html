**IMPORTANT NOTE FOR USERS:** Your privacy, especially concerning the documents you upload, is paramount to us. We have designed our systems with this principle in mind.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

a) Uploaded Files

  • File Content: When you upload files to use our Services (e.g., merge, split, compress PDFs), the content of those files is processed by our servers solely to perform the requested operation.
  • File Metadata: We may process metadata associated with your files (e.g., filename, file size, file type) to facilitate the Service.

Crucially: We do NOT store your uploaded files permanently. All uploaded files and any generated output files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers after a short retention period, typically one (1) hour after processing is complete or the download link is generated. We do not view, copy, or analyze the content of your files beyond the automated processing required for the tool's function.

b) Usage Data

When you access the Site and use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns. This may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URLs
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited on our Site
  • Tool usage (e.g., which tools were used, frequency)
  • Error logs (if technical issues occur during processing)

This data is generally aggregated and anonymized and is used for website analytics, improving our Services, monitoring performance, and diagnosing technical problems.

We may use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and other tracking technologies on the Site to help customize the Site and improve your experience. When you access the Site, your personal information is not collected through the use of tracking technology.

  • Types of Cookies We May Use:**
    • **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** Essential for the operation of the Site and Services (e.g., session management).
    • **Performance Cookies:** Collect information about how you use our Site (e.g., analytics).
    • **Functionality Cookies:** Remember choices you make to enhance user experience (e.g., language preferences).
    • **Advertising Cookies:** [**IF USING ADSENSE/OTHER AD NETWORKS, ADD THIS**] Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
